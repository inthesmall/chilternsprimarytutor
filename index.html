About me

My name is Jane and I am a qualified teacher with 15 years experience working with the 3-11 age range as a primary school teacher, SENCo and deputy headteacher.

I specialise in phonics and am passionate about ensuring every child can become a fluent, confident reader.

I know from experience that some children struggle to focus or keep up in a mainstream classroom but with high quality, tailored support they are able to thrive and achieve their full potential.

I personalise my approach to your child and will work with you to ensure the support is right for them.

For younger children this incorporates a play - based approach as this is how children learn best!
